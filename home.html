How to change password?
To change your password, please follow these step procedures. Goto account setting Click the "change password" tab Enter your current password. Enter your new password. Re-enter your new password to confirm. Save the new password. Please take note that your password will expire exaclty 1 month, please change your password regularly.
How to change my user photo
To change your user photo, please follow these step procedures. Goto account setting Click the "change avatar" tab select an image you want to upload Click the submit button to confirm image. Please take note that the system allows only an image file format with size not greater than 1mb.
